Catch The Eggs

    Game Rules:
  • Your goal is to catch as many eggs as possible.
  • 1. Blue Egg: Catching a blue egg gives you +1 point.
  • 2. Red Egg: If you catch the Red Egg, you lose 1 point
  • 3. Candie: If you catch the candy, you get 2 poins
  • 4. Time Limit: The game lasts for one minute.
    Your goal is to score as many points as possible during this time.
    • Baket movements:The Basket moves in all four directions using the arrow keys.
